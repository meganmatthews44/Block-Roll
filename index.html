Block and Roll

Click in gameboard to start!

How to Play:

Your objective is to hit the blocks with the ball.

Use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard to move your paddle left and right.

Use the paddle to hit the ball.

If the ball hits a block, the block will disappear and you score a point!

If the ball hits the ground, you lose!

If you hit all the blocks, you win the game!
